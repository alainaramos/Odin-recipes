• All purpose flour
  • Pumpkin Puree
  • 2 Eggs
  • Cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves mix
  • Vanilla
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  1. Add 2 cups flour, spice mix, brown sugar to medium mixing bowl
  2. In seperate bowl add pumpkin puree, 2 eggs, vanilla, and melted butter
  3. Mix dry indgredients to wet ingredients
  4. Heat skillet to 375, add 1/2 cup of batter to skillet
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on one side then flip and cook until golden brown
