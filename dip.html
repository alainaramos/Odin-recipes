Cream Cheese Dip

  • Philidelphia Cream Cheese
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Strawberries
  1. Allow Cream Cheese to warm to room temperature
  2. Put cream cheese in a bowl with two spoonfuls of powdered sugar
  3. Add 1 tsp vanilla
  4. Add 5 strawberries
  5. Mix together using a low to high setting on mixer until smooth
  6. Store in fridge for up to 4 days
